About Le Petit Sorin

A breif look into our history!

We bought Le Petit Sorin in 2017 and made the move to France from the UK in January 2018, leaving behind our jobs and the rat-race of London. We spent nearly 18 months restorting and renovating the farmhouse and opened in as a gite business in the summer of 2019, even though some of our friends and family thought we were crazy, we have never looked back. We have fallen for the beautiful region of the Charente Maritime, its sunny climate, the people, places, food and of course the wine! We love the tranquil location of Le Petit Sorin but also that it is within easy reach of some of the region's most fantastic cities and towns including Bordeaux, La Rochelle, Cognac and Blaye just to name but a few.

Reviews

Here are some reviews from our latest visitors

Jo W. - July 2019

Luxury Accommodation. Highly Recommended!

Just back from the most incredible week at Le Petit Sorin in Mortiers France with the wonderful hosts Emily and Andy. I couldn't have been happier or more impressed with this beautiful home and the hospitality and genuine 'here to help' attitude of Emily and Andy. From first communication to waving goodbye at the end of the week nothing was too much trouble. Pre-visit I was in touch with Emily with questions about what to do in the area, she came back promptly with ideas, distances, costs and links on how to contact and book. this included towns to visit, places to eat, local businesses such as a fabulous yoga and spa retreat just 10 mins away. A cognac vineyard, just 10 mins away where we had a private tour and tasting, contact details for us to book local restaurants in nearby Jonzac and Leoville and even a contact to book a hot air balloon for our special occasion. They gave us directions to a local supermarket where we were able to get everything we needed for our evenings in on the terrace by the pool and directions to a cafe for morning coffee opposite a quaint little Boulangerie for wonderful french bread, delicious pastries and the most marvellous fresh macaroons. Just heavenly. The details of the Gite shown on the site are accurate and it is as lovely as it looks, I would class this home as Luxury Accommodation and the attention to detail and the simple little touches are just lovely. All the bedrooms were beautifully decorated, most with en-suite bathrooms. 2 large and fully equipped kitchens made self-catering a breeze. We almost forgot Emily and Andy were there as they were so discreet and gave us privacy at all times, It was great to know they were just next door if we needed anything, keeping themselves to themselves and always with a friendly smile. Cleanliness 5*'s, Style 5*comfort 5*s, friendliness 5*s, location 5*s, surrounding area 5*s having to leave at the end of the week... absolute pants! Thank you, Emily and Andy we all had an amazing time and will see, you again at Le Petit Sorin.
